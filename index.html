Meet the Artist

Hi, I’m Lakshita — an undergraduate student and the heart behind LMANDALAS. What began as tiny doodles in my notebook slowly grew into a dream that now lives here. I started making mandalas in 2020, during a time when life slowed down and I discovered peace in patterns, colors, and delicate details. What began as a simple hobby soon became the place that grounds me — a quiet corner where I could express myself and bring calm into chaos. LMANDALAS is my way of sharing that feeling with the world. Every piece you see here is created with intention, patience, and a touch of joy — art that speaks softly, brings comfort, and adds a little sunshine to someone’s day. I’m still learning, still exploring, and still growing. But every design reminds me that small beginnings can become something beautiful when created with heart. This shop is my first step — a small start filled with passion. Each handmade item and every pattern is crafted with care, a spark of boldness, and the hope that it becomes a timeless little companion in your world. Thank you for being here. I’m just getting started — and I can’t wait to fill this space with new creations, ideas, and handmade wonders.
